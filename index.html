Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data from our app, please follow the instructions below:

Steps to Request Data Deletion:

  • Go to the Facebook account you used to log in to our app.
  • Navigate to "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings".
  • Click on "Apps and Websites" under "Permissions".
  • Find our app in the list and click on it.
  • Select "Remove" to disconnect the app from your Facebook account.
  • Contact us at solidauto2022@gmail.com with your user ID and request data deletion.

Once we receive your request, we will delete your data from our servers and notify you once the process is complete.
